DATA RETENTION POLICY
Acme Financial Services Ltd
Last Updated: January 2026

1. PURPOSE
This policy defines how Acme Financial Services retains, archives, and destroys personal data
in compliance with applicable regulations including GDPR and DORA.

2. RETENTION PERIODS
- Customer personal data: 7 years after relationship ends
- Transaction records: 10 years (regulatory requirement)
- Employee records: Duration of employment + 7 years
- Marketing consent records: 3 years or until withdrawn
- Incident logs: 5 years
- ICT system logs: 18 months minimum

3. DATA DESTRUCTION
Personal data is securely destroyed using DoD 5220.22-M standard for digital media.
Physical documents are shredded by certified vendor. Certificates of destruction are retained.

4. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS
Data is processed only within EU/EEA unless Standard Contractual Clauses are in place.
Current cross-border transfers: None.
